## Plan details

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Free">
    ### Free plan

    The Free plan gives you access to the essential tools at no cost:
    - **Control Sheet**: generate basic field reports with coordinates, notes, and photos
    - **Coordinate Converter**: convert between coordinate reference systems

    **Limits**: 2 PDFs per day, 20 PDFs per month, 5 stored projects.

    <Info>
      The Free plan is ideal for occasional use or to evaluate Topo.ninja before committing to a paid plan. No credit card required.
    </Info>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Standard">
    ### Standard plan

    The Standard plan unlocks the full suite of field reporting tools:
    - Everything in the Free plan
    - **Axis Control Sheet**: organize points by reference axes
    - **Signaletic Sheet**: compare project and implanted points
    - **Auto role assignment**: automatically classify points by naming convention
    - **Automatic pairing**: match implanted points to project points

    **Limits**: 25 PDFs per day, 25 PDFs per month, 100 stored projects.

    <Tip>
      The Standard plan is the best choice for surveyors who regularly produce stakeout control documents and need comparison tools.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Premium">
    ### Premium plan

    The Premium plan adds planning and advanced editing tools:
    - Everything in the Standard plan
    - **3D Resection Planner**: plan optimal total station positions with visibility and GDOP analysis
    - **Bulk Editor**: spreadsheet-style editing with copy-paste from Excel, fill handle, and sorting

    **Limits**: 100 PDFs per day, 100 PDFs per month, 300 stored projects.

    <Tip>
      If you do resection-based surveys or need the efficiency of spreadsheet editing for large point sets, Premium is the right fit.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enterprise">
    ### Enterprise plan

    The Enterprise plan is designed for teams and organizations:
    - Everything in the Premium plan
    - **Team management**: invite team members and manage accounts
    - **Shared projects**: collaborate on projects across your organization

    **Limits**: 100 PDFs per day, 100 PDFs per month, 300 stored projects.

    <Info>
      Enterprise plans include dedicated support and can be customized to your organization's needs. Contact us at [support@topo.ninja](mailto:support@topo.ninja) for details.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Changing plans

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your subscription page in Topo.ninja:

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **Subscription**
3. Choose the plan you want

Changes take effect immediately. When upgrading, you gain instant access to the new features. When downgrading, you retain access to your current plan until the end of the billing period.

## Free trial

A free trial is available so you can explore all features before subscribing. The trial gives you full access to Premium-level features for a limited time, with no credit card required to start.
