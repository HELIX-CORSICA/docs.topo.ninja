Topo.ninja is a web platform built for topographic surveying and construction professionals. It brings together a set of practical tools for generating field reports, performing coordinate conversions, and planning survey stations -- all from your browser.

## Why Topo.ninja?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="100% web-based" icon="globe">
    No installation required. Access all tools from your browser, on desktop or tablet. Your data stays in your browser -- nothing is sent to a server without your explicit action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Built for field professionals" icon="helmet-safety">
    Designed for land surveyors, geomatics engineers, and construction professionals. The tools address real-world needs: stakeout control sheets, project-vs-field comparison, coordinate system conversions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Job-site-ready PDFs" icon="file-pdf">
    Generate formatted, professional PDF reports in a few clicks. Control sheets, signaletic sheets, resection plans -- everything is ready to print or send.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transparent calculations" icon="square-root-variable">
    Every formula used in the computations is documented and accessible. You know exactly how your results are obtained.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The tools

### Stakeout

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Control Sheet" icon="clipboard-check" href="/en/tools/control-sheet">
    Generate field reports with your survey points, coordinates, notes, and photos. PDF export included.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Axis Control Sheet" icon="ruler-combined" href="/en/tools/axis-control-sheet">
    Create control sheets organized by reference axes, with automatic orthogonal distance computation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Measurement

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Signaletic Sheet Generator" icon="magnifying-glass-chart" href="/en/tools/signaletic-sheet">
    Compare your design (project) points with field-measured (implanted) points. Automatic pairing, photos, bulk editor, and PDF generation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Conversion

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Coordinate Converter" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left" href="/en/tools/coordinate-converter">
    Convert coordinates between different reference systems (Lambert 93, WGS84, UTM, etc.) with or without geoid grids.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Planning

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="3D Resection Planner" icon="location-crosshairs" href="/en/tools/3d-resection">
    Find the optimal total station position by analyzing 3D visibility, geometric quality (GDOP), and angular constraints.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
