This guide walks you from account creation to your first PDF document. In a few minutes, you will be up and running.

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [topo.ninja](https://topo.ninja) and click **Get started** or **Sign in**. You can create an account with your email address or sign in directly with Google.

    <Tip>
      Account creation is free and does not require a credit card. A 7-day Premium trial is automatically activated so you can explore all features.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Topo.ninja offers several plans tailored to your needs:

    | Plan | Access |
    |------|--------|
    | **Standard** | Control Sheet, Axis Control Sheet, Signaletic Sheet, Coordinate Converter |
    | **Premium** | + 3D Resection, advanced features |
    | **Enterprise** | Team management, shared accounts |

    You can change plans at any time from your subscription page. A 7-day free trial with Premium access is available to all new users.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Discover the dashboard">
    Once signed in, you land on the dashboard. It displays the list of available tools, organized by category:

    - **Stakeout**: Control Sheets and Axis Control Sheets
    - **Measurement**: Signaletic Sheet Generator
    - **Conversion**: Coordinate Converter
    - **Planning**: 3D Resection Planner

    Click a tool to open it. Each tool is independent: you can work on multiple projects in parallel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Let's use the **Control Sheet** as an example:

    1. Open the **Control Sheet** tool
    2. Fill in the project information: client, site, operator, reference
    3. Add your survey points (manually or by CSV import)
    4. Optionally add notes and photos to each point

    <Info>
      Your data is saved automatically in your browser. You can close the tab and come back later without losing your work.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate your first PDF">
    Once your points and project information are ready, click the **Generate PDF** button. The document is created directly in your browser and downloads automatically.

    The PDF contains:
    - Project information (client, site, date, operator)
    - The full table of points with their coordinates
    - A map preview (if available)
    - Notes and photos attached to points

    <Tip>
      Check the map preview before generating the PDF. If your points do not appear on the map, verify that the coordinates are in a recognized projected system (Lambert 93, UTM, etc.) and not in a local system without georeferencing.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
